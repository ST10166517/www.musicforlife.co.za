About Us

VISION: We believe Music to be a Universal means in developing a relatable connection with our youth in order to realistically impact their emotional, mental, spiritual, social, economic and academic stage. It is the vision of the society that through Educational, inspirational and character concentrated activities, we will empower at risk youth to become productive conscious citizens who appreciate their potential and strive towards their goals, while re-establishing that all things are possible to those who believe.

MISSION STATEMENT: Music for life is… A unified group of professionals whose interest shall be to provide an organization structure expressly for • Isolating the negatives • Exploring the positives • Summarizing the positives • Connect possibility to positivity • Alternative probability discussed • Planning and developing prospect • Entering prospect into community Through Educational, Inspirational and character concentrated activities, our Mission is to restore at risk youth from the chains of degradation, economic and social malformation and destructive resolutions. 03

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: The society is an after school program targeting at risk youth From ages 10-18, using the universal tool of music the Society will develop an invaluable connection in order to realistically impact the emotional, mental, spiritual, social, economic and academic state of our youth. Our approach is proactively building on the talents and abilities of the individual. Through Educational, Inspirational and character concentrated activities, we will empower at risk youth to become productive conscious citizens who appreciate their potential and strive towards their goals, while re-establishing that all things are possible to those who believe. The need to positively change our public Schools and communities is both Indisputable and absolute. Based on the facts and obvious destructive patterns among the youth we see the need for the schools and community to be restored from the chains of degradation. To implement and achieve our ultimate vision of empowering at Risk youth to become productive conscious citizens who appreciate their potential and strive towards their goals, Specifically the society hopes to obtain our own facility and collaborate with Public schools administration, student body and community of selected schools, government, non-government entities and media houses. We anticipate tremendous growth over the next 3-5 years.
